Personal Summary

With over 20 years experience building IT solutions across multiple industries and countries, I bring expertise in software engineering in general and specifically in the precise definition, elicitation, documentation and management of requirements in complex IT projects (CPRE Certified and IREB RE@Agile coauthor/examiner). That is, I offer real insights that can help your business discover the hidden needs and opportunities that can be addressed by innovative IT solutions, and proven skills in managing the organisational and technical complexity to implement such solutions.

Vision and Purpose

As a senior professional I am motivated both by the opportunity of applying battle-honed skills to new challenges within changing business environments and a rapidly evolving technological landscape, as well as by being able to share my insights and experience to motivate and educate others (see CYF). I believe in professionalising the discipline of requirements engineering and providing a certified path to support real career progression (see IREB). I believe in using technology to create innovative, well-designed and sustainable solutions that help businesses operate efficiently while positively impacting the people who use them and the societies in which they live.
